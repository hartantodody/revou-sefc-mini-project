About us

We are a small group of enthusiastic people that really enjoy teaching kids to learn about technology from the ground, until they got the logical-critical-mindset to improve the tech-industry for future generation.

We are actually based in Denpasar, Bali. And we have 2 other school in Jakarta and Surabaya, be sure to check our online classes!

And of course, we welcome you from any age to our StudioUs Club! In this place, you can learn the necessary things to maximizing your basic and fundamental for your programming skill. We have features that will help you to maximize your fundamental, such as :

  • Scheduled Interactive Live Videos
  • Quiz that will challenge you to complete some task
  • Online Text Editor for mix and matching your creativity

Don't miss it and be sure to check our News to join our classes!
